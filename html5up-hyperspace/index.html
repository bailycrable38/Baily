Who I am

My name is Baily Crable and I'm from Erie Pennsylvania. I really like birds.

Education

I went to undergrad at Mercyhurst University in Erie, where I earned my Bachelors of Science in Biology and I minored in chemistry.

Bird Research

My undergraduate thesis was looking at waterfowl and how they utilize different marsh areas in Erie, PA during different migration periods. Currently I am working on a behavioral study of white-throated sparrows

What I do

I am a biologist in training with a focus in ornithology. I am currently working on a Master's Degree at Austin Peay State University in Clarksville, Tennessee.

Ornithology

I took an ornithology class in undergrad right when the Covid-19 pandemic started. Birding became one of the only activities that I could do with everyone in quarantine which is how I fell in love with birding and studying birds.

Woltmann Lab

I joined the Woltmann lab at APSU in 2022 where I do white-throated sparrow research

White-throated Sparrows

My study species is currently the White-throated Sparrow (Zonotrichia albicollis). I am looking at how morph might play a role in interspecific dominance amongst Tennessee wintering bird species.

Animal Behavior

Behavioral ecology is one of my favorite fields of biology. My current thesis is a behavioral study looking at dominance amongst birds and how the two different morphs of White-throated Sparrow interact with other bird species.

Past research

I have past research experience working with Chinese Mystery Snails looking at their water depth preferences. I have also done reserach in undergrad looking at erosion of Erie bluffs and looking at emerald ash borer in long term forest plots.

In the future

After I finish my Master's I am hoping to gain some more field experience in ornithology (as well as other fields). I'm especially interested in conservation studies as well as more behavioral studies.
